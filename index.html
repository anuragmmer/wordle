How to Play

Guess the WORDLE in 6 tries. Each guess must be a valid 5-letter word. After each guess, the tiles reveal how close you were.

W
W is in the correct spot.
I
I is in the word but wrong spot.
U
U is not in the word.

Play unlimited rounds — a fresh word every time you start a new game. Your stats are saved locally on your device.
